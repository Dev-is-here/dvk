Introduction

Divyansh Kacholia (born 5th March,1997) also known as DVK the BeatBoxer is a beatboxer from Jaipur India.

He was a finalist in the reality singing competition ‘Dil Hai Hindustani -2’. He is the touring artiste with Sunidhi Chauhan and Shaan. He has been instrumental in the making beatboxing a mainsteam artform in India. Divyansh has taken beatboxing beyond the Hip Hop genre and has added a folksy element to his music which is a tribute to his hometown.

Life & Career

Divyansh Kacholia was born in Kota, Rajasthan to Santosh and Vinod Kacholia. He did his schooling from Maheshwari Public School in Jaipur.

Divyansh began beatboxing at the age of 13, when he’d tap school benches and make music. It was only a year later that a senior at school told him that the way he made music was called Beatboxing. When he was 16, at a local music store, a band saw him beatboxing and invited him to perform at a gig with them. He did multiple gigs with the band and even began performing at hi school. Before his 12th Board Examination, he auditioned for Rajasthan’s Got Talent and made it to the finale.

He pursued his undergraduate education in DCAC (Delhi College of Arts and Commerce), Delhi University and performed in colleges and participated in Beatbox Battles and even won a few. Divyansh’s career took a turn for the better when he met the 2012 World Beatboxing Champion, Skiller and that’s when Divyansh decided to go all in and began taking Beatboxing seriously, putting in 16 hours of practice everyday. His friend, Sahil Mandan began managing Divyansh and wanted to build ‘DVK’ as a brand. In 2016, he was selected among India’a Top 32 beatboxers.

During his final year of college, Divyansh was selected as a contestant for ‘Dil Hai Hindustani – 2’ but it was when he reached the finale of TV reality show “Dil Hai Hindustani” that his professional life changed. Judge from the show, Sunidhi Chauhan invited Divyansh to join her UK Tour, he also performed with Shaan at the Kala Ghoda Arts Fesitval where he got to the opportunity to perform solo in the spotlight. He was also a guest singer on AR Rahman’s YouTube music reality show Arrived. He has performed with indie artistes like MIDIval Panditz and Karsh Kale. He has collaborated with well known brands like Colgate, Pringles, Lenskart, HP and many more to create feet thumping anthems.
